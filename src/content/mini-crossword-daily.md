Mini Crossword Daily challenges you to stay focused while solving crossword puzzles under time pressure. New puzzles are released daily at no cost. The game tests whether you have the skill and speed needed to beat the clock.

As time ticks down, the screen changes color to heighten the challenge — adding a visual layer of intensity to every solve. Track your win streak and see how many days in a row you can conquer the puzzle before time expires.

Available on both the Apple App Store and Google Play, Mini Crossword Daily is always free to play. Whether you're a crossword veteran or a casual puzzler, the mini format keeps things quick, fun, and endlessly replayable.
